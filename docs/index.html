FOR EVE ONLINE FLEET COMMANDERS

Run the fleet,
not the spreadsheet.

FCAT is a desktop command deck that reads your live fleet from ESI and your game logs — roster & roles, system intel, ping/MOTD building, and on-screen alerts when it matters.

Everything an FC reaches for, in one window

Intel

Constellation map, hot-system heat (kills · jumps · ratting delta), and a live feed that merges zKill kills with your in-game intel channel.

Ping & MOTD

Compose a clean formup ping and a fleet MOTD from shared fields — clickable channel and anchor links — then push it straight to the fleet over ESI.

Alerts & overlay

Tackle, cap-out, boost-lost and logi-chain alerts — in-app and on a draggable always-on-top overlay that sits over the game.

⧉

Fleet Ops

Live roster and wing/squad hierarchy, role detection by hull, composition advisories (logi % · tackle · boost), a cap-chain readout, and one-click moves/kicks.

Honest about what EVE actually exposes

FCAT only claims what the data supports. No guessing, no fake telemetry.

  • Reads your live fleet, roles by hull, locations & system intel via ESI.
  • Reads warp-scramble (tackle) and cap-out lines from your combat log.
  • Reads boost charges from the in-game boost channel you're in.
  • Can't see webs, neuts, ECM, damps or paints — EVE never logs them.
  • Can't read Local or d-scan automatically — those are paste-driven.
